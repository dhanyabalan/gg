Welcome to Thiruvanandhapuram


Thiruvananthapuram offers a number of places to see and things to do in to its visitors for an unforgettable Kerala holiday experience. Some of the best things to do in and around the city are to relax on Varkala Beach, which is located on the outskirts. One can also visit the nearby Vishnu Temple here, which is believed to be about 2000 years old. Don't forget to take a dip in the water here, as it is supposed to absolve all the sins. Do visit Kalamachal, a tiny hill station located in the Thiruvananthapuram district. It is a lesser-known destination in Kerala and is perfect for those who want to spend their time away from the crowds. At magic academy learn about the evolution of magic tricks. Indulge in healing Ayurvedic treatments at prominent centres in Thiruvananthapuram. A laid-back wooden boat ride to Kovalam's Main Beach is also an ideal thing to do along with indulging in sports water sports activities like parasailing, paragliding, swimming, surfing, skiing, catamaran ride, and scuba diving among others at Kovalam Beach. The visitors also have the choice to imbibe the spiritual essence of life by practicing a yoga practice amidst the verdant greenery of the city. Yet another must do includes hopping in a City Day Tour Bus. Those with great interest in music can learn the classical music at Margi Kathakali School. There is also option for learn the techniques of martial arts at CVN Kalari Sangham. Also, tourist can plan a visit to Thenmala, which India's first planned eco-tourism centre and is 70 km away from Thiruvananthapuram. As far as sightseeing in the city is considered, the visitors have many options like Kanakakkunnu Palace, Sree Chithra Art Gallery, The Napier Museum, Akkulam Lake,Science and Technology Museum, Sree Chithra Art Gallery amongst many others.


01 Napier Museum



Named after Lord Napier, this art and history museum was established in 1855. An exemplary example of gothic architecture-style, Napier Museum boasts greatly of its natural air conditioning system.... It houses an exceptional collection of ornaments, bronze idols, chariots, and ivory carvings. The museum offers insight into the rich and glorious culture and history of Kerala. The great contribution made by Rajput, Mughal, and Tanjore schools of art along with the skillful work by several Chinese, Japanese, and Tibetan artists can be found in here. The Japanese leather figures depicting the epics of Mahabharata and Ramayana catch the eyes of tourists. Napier Museum is certainly one of the most visited tourist places in Trivandrum. Situated in the Museum compound, the ground floor of this place houses India’s oldest zoological garden. Another important feature is Sree Chithra Art Gallery, established by Maharaja Chithira Thirunalin in 1935. The gallery contains epic works from much famed Raja Ravi Varma and Nicholas Roerich. Amid a well-laid-out garden, the zoological park houses a lake and boat club. The park offers an excellent day outing for children and adults alike. It boasts of 75 different species of animals and birds from India, Ethiopia, and Australia. It is also home to the Lion-tailed Macaque, Nilgiri Tahr, Manipur deer, Indian Rhino, Nilgiri Langur, Asiatic Lion and the Royal Bengal Tiger. There is prominent fauna found here along with habitats of giraffe, hippopotamus, zebra, and Cape buffalo.


02 Magic Planet



Magic Planet- the planets first magic house which is entirely a new concept with completely magic themed park located at the Kinfra Film and Video Park, unmatched and innovative facility was designed to torch of Science and elixir the magic of young generation, dedicated entirely to the improvement of India’s street magicians. ... The complex, is spread over 75 acres, will house numerous attractions including a museum, an underground tunnel, a mirror maze, shadow plays, a virtual supermarket to build mathematical skills, a children’s park, a food court, theaters for live performances and an R&D center. How to Reach: This facility can be reached by any local transport. Timings: Opened daily and conducts full day programs. Price: Approximate price starts from 250 INR.
